About Me

I'm Brandon Reyes. I am a data and analytics professional with over eight years of experience in turning complex data into clear, actionable insights. As a Microsoft Certified Fabric Analytics Engineer (DP-600) and Power BI Data Analyst (PL-300), I have a deep expertise in the entire business intelligence lifecycle, from data preparation to final visualization. My core skill is transforming scattered and inefficient processes into automated, data-driven solutions. I am highly proficient in using Power Query for complex ETL, building scalable data models using star schema best practices, and writing advanced DAX to uncover key business metrics. My background also includes extensive experience in process automation using tools like Power Automate and DocuWare. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work on high-impact projects. A highlight was developing a crucial Power BI report for the City of Laredo, which enabled stakeholders to see the real-time financial impact of complex union negotiations. I was also honored to be an invited speaker at the U.S. Census Bureau's Opportunity Project Summit to provide expert feedback on national data solutions. Beyond the technical aspects, I am passionate about empowering people with data. My experience as an instructor has taught me how to make complex topics simple and to build solutions that are not only powerful but also intuitive and easy to use.

My approach is different because I'm a teacher at heart. With hundreds of hours of teaching experience, I don't just deliver a technical solution; I empower you and your team to understand and use it effectively. As a Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst (PL-300) and Fabric Analytics Engineer (DP-600), you can be confident that your project is built on a foundation of industry best practices.
